Game Overview

Genshin Impact is an open-world action role-playing game developed and published by miHoYo. Released in September 2020, the game has garnered a massive following worldwide due to its beautiful graphics, engaging gameplay, and rich storyline.

Players explore the fantasy world of Teyvat, where they control one of four interchangeable characters in a party, using elemental magic and character-switching to combat enemies and solve puzzles.
